My favourite subject.
Throughout my childhood I have loved to play and watch various sports. In all sports that I watched, I was always fascinated by the use of interesting graphs which displayed performance of players, especially in cricket analysis. Though I found it a challenging topic to study, I was determined to build a concept of how numbers could be used to make different types of Graphs. Correlating the given data with statistics used in matches helped me grasp the concept. I was thrilled to study the topic of Graphs including bar charts, box whisker plots and stem leaf diagrams. I have been using these graphs to analyse scores of the friendly matches that I play with my classmates. I have gained skills in inference and assessing impact of numerous factors. I feel like I have a career prospect in a field which involves statistics.
